About Us

Our Mission

Athar was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to connect environmentally conscious consumers with sustainable local businesses. We believe that small changes in our daily purchasing decisions can lead to significant positive impacts on our planet.

Our Impact

Since our launch, Athar users have supported over 500 eco-friendly businesses, reduced carbon emissions by an estimated 1,200 tons, and helped divert thousands of pounds of waste from landfills. Join us in creating a greener, more sustainable future!
